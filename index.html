Rules

Blackjack is a game that involves 2 players. The dealer deals 2 cards to himself and the player at the start of the game.

The goal is to get to 21 points. The winner is the person who is closest to 21 points. If both players go over 21 points, they both lose.

If one person goes over 21 and the other person is under 21, the person under 21 points wins.

Jack/Queen/King counts as 10 points. Ace counts as 1 or 11 points depending on the player.

The player must decide if they want to hit(gain an additional card) or stand(don't want an additional card to boost their points).
